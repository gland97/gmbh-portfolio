ABOUT ME

This portfolio is the culmination of eight months of work towards a goal to become a product designer. Being home from March until my graduation from BU this past May I jumped into starting a new Minecraft Server Hosting platform. Through months of determination to learn the ins and outs of server hosting, Minecraft, and my role in all of it, I found an area that I am constantly drawn to (and a little obsessed with) – product design.

I hope my portfolio gives you a sense of where I am in my journey, the hard work it took to get here and most of all, the enjoyment and satisfaction that I’ve felt designing a product people use everyday.
